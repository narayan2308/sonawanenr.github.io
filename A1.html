Prediction of Landslides using ANN

  • Narayan Sonawane
  • Domain: Geotechnical Engineering
  • Duration : 1 YEARS

In this project we have aimed to apply Artificial intelligence, a modern technology for computation for solving various geotechnical problems and to get effective error free solution.

Problem Statement-STUDY ON APPLICATIONS OF ARTIFICIAL NEURAL NETWORK IN GEOTECHNICAL ENGINEERING

Objectives - • To collect and study previous researches on applications of ANN for prediction of landslides. • To learn various tools of artificial neural network for landslide studies. • To develop a template for landslide studies.

The results show that artificial neural network can be effectively used for prediction of different applications of geotechnical engineering and for the prediction of landslides. We will learn to apply various ANN tools for the prediction of landslides by analysis disaster data in the specified region. And the objective and scope of the project is achieved

Future Scope

To apply various ANN tools for prediction of landslides by analysing disaster data in the specified region.
