Designing water distribution system for Kati Village

  • Narayan Sonawane
  • Domain: Environmental and water resource engineering
  • Duration : 0.5 YEARS

For capstone I have selected my own native village asi have seen my village going through the droughtsince my childhood. in this projectI did visits and ground work to study the current water distribution system of Kati village and found out the problemssuch asirregular watersupply, insufficient water availability, inefficientsystem To solve these problems I have redesigned and optimized the water distribution system for alandiregion. For this I have done the field visit of 7 days and collected data from villegers and government authorites to identify the problems in existing water distribution system.

Problems Identified: • Water supplies is insufficient • Busting pipes happens due to high pressure very often resulting irregular supply of water • Due to nonfunctionality of WTP water quality is bad and not suitable for drinking • Due to load-shedding of electricity the village dosent get full water intake assigned from source resulting insufficient water available for supply

Objectives - • To collect and study previous researches on applications of ANN for prediction of landslides. • To learn various tools of artificial neural network for landslide studies. • To develop a template for landslide studies.

By considering above calculation and with help of collected data i have redesigned the water distribution system which eliminate all the existing problmes in current water distribution sytem of kati villege using waterGems.

Future Scope

To propose the design to the government and execute it in the villege to make kati villege free from all the water scarcity and problems
